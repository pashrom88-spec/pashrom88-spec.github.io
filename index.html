PeerForge was born out of a simple frustration: finding people to study with shouldn't require posting in a 500-person Discord server and hoping for the best. We wanted something that matched you with classmates covering the same material, at the same time — without friction.

We started as a side project between two computer science students pulling all-nighters before finals. Today PeerForge connects students across dozens of universities in six countries. We're still students at heart, which means we build for the actual experience — not the idealized one.

PeerForge is free for individual students, always. No ads, no data selling, no paywalled core features. We keep the lights on through optional Campus plans for institutions that want analytics and LMS integrations.

This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what rights you have over it. If you have questions, email us at privacy@peerforge.app.

01 · Who we are

PeerForge Inc. ("PeerForge", "we", "us") operates the website at peerforge.netlify.app and all associated services (collectively, the "Service").

For GDPR purposes, PeerForge Inc. is the data controller. Our data protection contact is privacy@peerforge.app.

02 · What we collect

We collect only the minimum data required to run the Service:

Account & sign-up data

University email address (required for verification), display name (optional), and the timestamp of sign-up. We never collect a full legal name, date of birth, or payment details on the free tier.

Content you post

Study group listings, subjects, availability, descriptions, and community comments that you voluntarily submit. This content is visible to other signed-in users.

Usage & analytics data

Page views, session duration, and feature interactions collected via Google Analytics (GA4). This data is anonymised and aggregated. We do not enable Google Signals or demographic reporting.

Technical & device data

IP address (truncated), browser type, operating system, and referring URL collected automatically by our hosting provider (Netlify). This is standard server-log data retained for 30 days.

Locally stored preferences

Language preference, design customiser settings, and your liked-comment IDs are stored only in your browser's localStorage. We never transmit this data to our servers.

03 · How we use your data

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • → To verify you are a student via your university email and allow you to post and join study groups.
  • → To display your posted groups and comments to other students using the Service.
  • → To send you transactional emails (e.g. email confirmation, group-match notifications) where you have opted in.
  • → To understand how students use PeerForge so we can improve the product (aggregated analytics only).
  • → To detect and prevent spam, abuse, or misuse of the platform.

We do not use your data to serve targeted advertisements, sell it to third parties, or build individual behavioural profiles.

04 · Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland, we rely on the following legal bases under GDPR Article 6:

Contract Providing the core Service — matching students, storing groups — requires processing your email and posted content.
Consent Marketing emails and optional analytics. You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing us or clicking unsubscribe.
Legitimate interest Security monitoring, fraud prevention, and aggregated product analytics that do not override your fundamental rights.

05 · Sharing & third parties

We share data with the following third-party service providers only, each bound by Data Processing Agreements:

Provider Purpose Data shared
Netlify Hosting & CDN Server logs, IP address
Google Analytics Usage analytics Anonymised page & event data
Google Fonts Typography Your IP address on font load

We will disclose data if required by law or to protect against fraud, but we will notify you where legally permitted to do so. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

06 · Cookies & local storage

PeerForge uses browser localStorage (not cookies) for session preferences. Google Analytics sets the following first-party cookies:

Cookie Purpose Expiry
_ga Distinguish unique visitors 2 years
_ga_* Session state for GA4 2 years

You can disable analytics cookies at any time via your browser settings or by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on.

07 · Data retention

  • → Account data — retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.
  • → Posted groups & comments — retained until you delete them or close your account.
  • → Server logs — retained for 30 days by Netlify, then automatically purged.
  • → Analytics data — aggregated and retained for up to 14 months per Google Analytics default settings.
  • → Local storage — stored in your browser indefinitely until you clear browser data. We cannot access or delete this remotely.

08 · Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have some or all of the following rights:

Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

Erasure

Request deletion of your account and associated data.

Rectification

Correct inaccurate data we hold about you.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Objection

Object to processing based on legitimate interests.

Withdraw consent

Opt out of marketing emails at any time, no questions asked.

To exercise any right, email privacy@peerforge.app. We will respond within 30 days. If you are unhappy with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

09 · Children & students

PeerForge is intended for university students aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at privacy@peerforge.app and we will delete the account promptly.

We require a university .edu or institutional email address to sign up, which acts as a reasonable age and enrollment verification mechanism. PeerForge does not fall under FERPA as we are not an educational institution, but we apply equivalent data minimisation principles to all student data.

10 · Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • → TLS encryption for all data in transit (HTTPS enforced site-wide)
  • → Input sanitisation to prevent XSS and injection attacks
  • → Rate limiting on comment and group submissions
  • → No third-party ad scripts or tracking pixels beyond those listed above

No system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33.

11 · International data transfers

PeerForge operates globally. Your data may be processed in the United States, Canada, or the European Economic Area. Where data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or the UK International Data Transfer Agreement where applicable. Netlify and Google maintain active certifications under applicable data transfer frameworks.

12 · Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email (if you have an account) or by posting a prominent notice on the site at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

Continued use of PeerForge after the effective date of any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. Previous versions of this policy are available on request.

faq

Is PeerForge actually free?
Yes — free forever for individual students. No trial periods, no credit card required, no surprise paywalls. We sustain the platform through institutional Campus Plan subscriptions.
How does the Campus Plan work?
Universities pay a flat annual fee that covers every enrolled student. Students get all Campus features automatically — they never see a bill. Contact us to get a quote for your institution.
What email notifications will I get?
On the Free plan: instant notification when someone joins a group you posted. On Campus plan: also a weekly digest of new groups in your subject area and alerts for groups in your major.
Can I unsubscribe from notifications?
Yes. Every email has a one-click unsubscribe, and you can manage notification preferences in your account settings at any time.
Do you offer student discounts?
The Free plan IS the student discount — it's zero dollars. If your university hasn't signed up for Campus Plan yet, send them our way and we'll expedite their demo.
What happens to my data?
We never sell your data. See our for full details. Campus and Enterprise plans include a signed Data Processing Agreement.
